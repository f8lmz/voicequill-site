Why VoiceQuill

The voice for creators who’d rather not be the voice.

Most people don’t need a sprawling voice platform. They need a clean way to turn a script into narration they own — and publish.

01 — Fast

Fast by architecture.

VoiceQuill runs on your Mac. No upload cycle. No rendering queue. Type, generate, adjust, export — then move on.

02 — Private

Private by default.

No account. No login. No cloud upload. Your scripts never leave your Mac — because there’s no server to send them to. Private by architecture, not by promise.

03 — Yours

Yours by design.

10 voices, each tunable for pitch, warmth, and tone. Mark your pauses and stress so the delivery is yours — not a flat read.
